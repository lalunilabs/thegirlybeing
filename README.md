# ✿ The Girly Being

A beautiful, feminine blog space exploring womanhood, psychology, cycles, and the quiet power of being a woman.

## What's Inside

- **Homepage** (`index.html`) - Editorial-style landing with pillars, featured articles, and newsletter capture
- **Articles Page** (`all-articles.html`) - All posts organized with filters and beautiful typography
- **Individual Posts** - Template-based articles with drop caps, blockquotes, and pull quotes
- **Newsletter Integration** - "It's a Girl Thing" bi-weekly newsletter capture throughout

## Design Philosophy

- **Typography**: Playfair Display (headings) + DM Sans (body) + Quicksand (labels)
- **Color Palette**: Soft blush pinks, deep plum, mint, butter yellow, lilac
- **Aesthetic**: Editorial, airy, feminine without being childish
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first with sticky sidebar on desktop

## Key Features

- ✦ Drop caps on article intros
- ✦ Blockquotes and pull quotes with beautiful styling
- ✦ Text highlights (pink, rose, mint, butter)
- ✦ Category filters (Mind Mechanics, The Becoming, Energy Shift, etc.)
- ✦ Author mystery (anonymous voice)
- ✦ "My POV" section at end of posts
